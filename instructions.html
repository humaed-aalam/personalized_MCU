About Personalized Marvel Cinematic Universe

  • It is a fantasy team making website where you can have your marvel heroes and villains teamed up according to your choice.

  • Go through the strengths and weaknesses of every character, click on Know your heroes/Know your villains tab on home page accoordingly.

  • This will open a new webpage where you can hover over the images to know the name of the character and when you click them, the character's information page will open up.

  • After going through information of characters, click on create your team either on Home page or on characters' information page.

  • This will open a new webpage, prompt will open up asking you to enter name of your desired heroes and villains (two each).

  • Please enter names carefully in all lower case (for example if you desired to choose Thor then you have to type thor)

  • If the name of your desired hero contains more than one word then simply add a space between the two names (for example if your desired character is Captain America or Doctor Doom then you have to type captain america or doctor doom respectively)

  • Based on some calculations (which are based on performance chart shown to you in character's information page) you will be shown scores of both teams.

  • Check whether your fantasized team of heroes will be able to beat your fantasized team of villains or not !

  • Kindly note that this fantasy based team creating website is based on my fantasy and there is big chance that we might be disagreeing on some points.

  • Best of luck !
